❓ FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel my subscription? ▼
You can cancel your subscription anytime from your account settings. Go to Settings → Subscription → Cancel Plan. Your access will continue until the end of your billing period.
What file types can I upload? ▼
We support PDF, TXT, DOC, DOCX, PPT, PPTX, XLS, XLSX, RTF, and many more formats. Simply drag and drop your file or click to browse.
Is there a free trial available? ▼
Yes! We offer a free trial for our Premium plan. You can cancel anytime during the trial and you won't be charged.
Can I access documents offline? ▼
Premium subscribers can download documents for offline reading on our mobile apps for Android and iOS.
How is copyright protected? ▼
We take copyright seriously. All uploads must comply with our Copyright Policy. You must own the copyright to any document you share. We have a robust DMCA process to handle infringement reports.
What devices are supported? ▼
ALL IN ONE is available on the web, Android, and iOS. Your reading progress and library sync seamlessly across all your devices.
